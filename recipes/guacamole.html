Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a spicy Mexican paste made from crushed avocado and various seasonings, usually including onions, peppers, garlic and tomatoes. Guacamole is used as a condiment, an ingredient, and as an appetizer when served with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • Juice of ½ lime
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ chopped onion

Steps

  1. Gently slice the avocados and remove pit.
  2. Smash the avocados in a bowl till smooth.
  3. Add tomatoes and onion.
  4. Squeeze lime on the mixture.
  5. Add salt and stir.
