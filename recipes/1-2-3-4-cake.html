1-2-3-4 Cake

Description

A traditional American recipe for a 1-2-3-4 cake, a simple form of yellow cake. The name comes from the list of basic ingredients that follow below:

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 4 eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 pinches salt
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 °C.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until fluffy.
  3. Beat in the vanilla and eggs, one at a time, ensuring the batter is well mixed after each egg.
  4. Gradually sift in the dry ingredients, alternating with small additions of the milk.
  5. Transfer to a greased and floured baking pan.
  6. Bake at 175 °C, until a skewer inserted shows no moist crumbs.
